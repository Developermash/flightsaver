To contact support, send an email to developermash@mail.ru. If your question is related to a specific flight, include the flight number, date, departure and arrival airports, device model, and iOS version. Please do not send full boarding passes or personal documents unless they are required to resolve the issue.

How we can help

Boarding passes and import

We can help with scanning boarding passes, importing photos, PDFs, and files, and adding flights manually.

Flights and aviation data

We can explain why flight data may be unavailable, differ between providers, or update with a delay.

iCloud and flight history

We can help with Flightsaver sync, import, export, and flight history cleanup questions.

Premium and purchases

We can help with trial access, subscriptions, purchase restoration, and Premium feature access.

Subscription and purchase restoration

If you have an active subscription but Premium features are not unlocked, open Flightsaver, go to Premium settings, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID that was used for the purchase.

Payment, cancellation, refunds, and subscription management are handled through the App Store and the user's Apple ID. Flightsaver does not receive or store payment card details.

Data deletion

You can clear your flight history in the app settings. If iCloud sync is enabled, deletion may sync across your devices through iCloud. If you need help with data deletion or privacy questions, contact developermash@mail.ru.
